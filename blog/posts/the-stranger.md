# Side project meets personal hobby 
I've been attending a book club organized here in Taipei once a month. This book club is called [Read Around the World](https://meetu.ps/e/PXqBc/LCysF/i) and we select a book that is manageable to be read within a month and gather in a coffee shop to discuss about the book. So far I've attended three events and today I will be attending my fourth one. This month we have been reading about Albert Camus' book "The Stranger."
