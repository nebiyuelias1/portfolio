# Side project meets personal hobby 
I've been attending a book club organized here in Taipei once a month. This book club is called [Read Around the World](https://meetu.ps/e/PXqBc/LCysF/i) and we select a book that is manageable to be read within a month and gather in a coffee shop to discuss about the book. So far I've attended three events and today I will be attending my fourth one. This month we have been reading Albert Camus' book "The Stranger."
<img width="542" height="962" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3132fc42-d6dc-4a75-9f4e-3f0e0f5e154a" />
But at the same time I was building a book reading app that is focused on Ethiopian literature. I was trying to focus solely on Ethiopian market but after attending the book club a couple of times I was thinking why not make this app focused on book clubs as well. Therefore, the last month I pivoted and made it my mission to customize the app to book clubs. So I added features for creating book clubs, book reading sessions and posting discussion questions.
